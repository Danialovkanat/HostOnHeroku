About Us

We are a single center for online education "STADIUM", we are actively moving forward, offering a new approach to obtaining additional education and participating in the promotion of distance learning, which is gaining an increasing audience. For 2020 we: we have a perpetual license for training in working specialties, professional retraining and advanced training in short-term courses; We try to combine traditional theoretical training with progressive teaching technologies, monitor changes in professional standards and modernize the educational space. Development taking into account the trends of the time allows us to provide high-quality training for specialists who are competitive in the labor market.
